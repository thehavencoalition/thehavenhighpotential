# The Haven Co: Town and Upgrade Roadmap

This roadmap turns every major game spot into a five-step visual progression. Each fifth level should trigger a story quest, introduce a person, or require a found piece before the upgrade installs.

Town concept art:

- `assets/mockups/concept-town-map.png`

## Town Districts

### Haven Bar

1. Corner Storefront - one bar counter, a tiny line, and the first regulars.
2. Bud Bar Stools - seats, displays, and steadier promo traffic.
3. Lounge Glow - the shop starts reading as a social club.
4. Event Bar - stage nights and snack rushes make the storefront busy all day.
5. High Society Flagship - the original location becomes the brand anchor.

### Grow Warehouse

1. Garage Grow - starter beds and clamp lights.
2. Raised Warehouse - cleaner rows, wider paths, better airflow.
3. Auto Irrigation - visible water lines and timers.
4. Processing Wing - trim tables, drying racks, and harvest crates.
5. Signature Lab - genetics gear for the Haven signature strain chapter.

### Town Square

1. Flyer Table - the first public pop-up.
2. Weekend Pop-Up - repeat crowds and stronger reputation.
3. Street Fair Booth - events spill into town.
4. Block Party Permit - bigger crowds with more noise risk.
5. High Society Festival - public celebration hub.

### City Hall

1. Intake Window - basic paperwork and lower panic.
2. Permit Binder - compliance lowers inspection pressure.
3. Council Ally - political favor opens story choices.
4. Zoning Review - expansion approvals become a visible goal.
5. Expansion Approval - gateway to new Haven locations.

### Community Park

1. Cleanup Table - goodwill and lower heat.
2. Picnic Spot - neighbors see the Haven supporting the block.
3. Community Garden - trust-building counterweight to legal pressure.
4. Neighbor Day - better reviews and safer events.
5. Green Ribbon Park - the neighborhood publicly backs the brand.

### Delivery Yard

1. Snack Drop - prevents snack bar stalls.
2. Supply Racks - spare shelves and coolers.
3. Part Crates - upgrade pieces appear naturally during story gates.
4. Loading Bay - supports bigger events and snack rushes.
5. Regional Route - ready for multi-location expansion.

### Mural Wall

1. Primer Wall - blank brand canvas.
2. Neon Tag - purple and cyan street-art identity.
3. Community Wall - the mural becomes a local landmark.
4. Photo Spot - influencers and VIPs boost reputation.
5. High Society Gateway - signature visual for the district.
