# The Haven Co: High Society Prototype

This is the first playable browser prototype for **The Haven Co: High Society**.

Open `index.html` directly, or use the local preview URL from Codex when it is running.

## What Is Playable

- Pick current seeds from the Seed Vault.
- Plant selected seeds in the grow side.
- Watch plant slots progress through growth stages.
- Harvest ready plants into strain-specific raw product.
- Stock the customer counter with a strain mix.
- Serve walking customers for money and reputation.
- Use Snack Rush after Snack Bar Level 1 to actively sell snacks and move the munchies chapter forward.
- Upgrade grow lights, plant rows, counter, lounge, snack bar, stage, and security.
- Hire staff to automate planting, harvesting, stocking, and heat control.
- Run stage events after Stage Gear Level 1.
- Design the owner avatar with skin, hair, outfit, accessory, name, and personality style.
- Unlock extra avatar cosmetics through story progress, events, reputation, and special encounters.
- Track Vibe, Munchies, Political Favor, Heat, cash, level, and reputation.
- Watch the Legal warning light shift between Green, Caution, Warning, and Spy Watch.
- Follow the Mascot Tutorial card for the next useful action.
- Progress through story quests.
- Continue into repeatable Daily Loop quests after the current story chapters.
- Trigger story gates every five upgrade levels.

## Story Gate Rule

Every upgrade has a normal progression rhythm:

1. Levels 1 through 4 are normal paid upgrades.
2. Level 5 triggers a story quest with an NPC.
3. The player must meet someone, collect pieces, serve customers, harvest product, run an event, or control Heat.
4. Completing the quest allows the upgrade to install.
5. The same rule repeats at levels 10, 15, 20, and beyond.

This keeps upgrades from feeling like only a currency sink. The business grows because the player builds relationships and finds better pieces.

## Main Controls

- **Plant Seeds:** fills empty plant slots using the selected Seed Vault strain.
- **Harvest Ready:** collects harvest-ready plants.
- **Stock Counter:** moves harvested strain batches to the customer counter mix.
- **Snack Rush:** unlocks at Snack Bar Level 1, serves snack customers, and restocks snacks when empty.
- **Run Event:** starts an entertainment event once the stage is unlocked.
- **Hire Staff:** hires the next useful employee role.
- **Story:** reopens the current story or gate dialogue.
- **Guide:** reopens the opening tutorial.
- **Seed Vault:** selects seeds, shows fictional strain stats, and displays unlock requirements.
- **Owner card:** changes the player avatar and gives a light personality bonus.
- **Style Closet:** shows locked and unlocked cosmetic rewards with requirements.

## Seed Vault

The prototype includes 63 seed entries inspired by public strain-name databases. The names are used as collectible game flavor; grow speed, yield, price, Vibe, Munchies, color, and unlock rules are fictional gameplay balance.

Starter seeds:

- Blue Dream
- OG Kush
- Sour Diesel

Additional seeds unlock through level, reputation, story chapters, events, upgrade gates, and late-game progress. The counter now tracks a strain mix, so better seeds increase the value of customer sales.

## Current Tone Direction

The story direction is mostly comedy, with friendly neighborhood warmth and a little drama from rivals, city pressure, and suspicious customers. Legal pressure is currently a warning-light system rather than a constant punishment system. When Heat gets high, an occasional Secret Shopper can appear and test whether the shop is stocked and orderly.

## Avatar Progression

The starter creator includes basic skin tones, hair, outfits, accessories, and personality styles. Higher-tier cosmetics unlock through play:

- Neon Streak: complete Grow Lights Level 5.
- VIP Velvet: complete Build the Vibe.
- Comedy Jacket: run the first event.
- Paperwork Blazer: unlock the Security Desk.
- VIP Shades: reach 30 reputation.
- Mic Charm: complete The Stage Is Set.
- Permit Pin: handle a Secret Shopper.
